Community Newsletter & Updates

Renovation Project Approved

The municipal council has officially approved the development plan for the new Central Green Park. The construction is scheduled to start next month and will feature modern walking paths, safe playground areas, and a dedicated community gardening space.

Annual Food Drive Success

Thanks to all our generous neighbors, this year's Annual Charity Food Drive broke all previous records. We collected over 1,500 pounds of non-perishable food products, which have been successfully distributed to local shelters and food pantries.

Volunteers Wanted for Summer Fest

Planning for the annual Summer Festival is underway. The event committee is looking for energetic volunteers to help with stage setups, game stall coordination, and guest welcoming services. If you would like to help, please sign up through our registration form.
